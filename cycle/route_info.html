Discover Amsterdam

The bicycle route Around Amsterdam leads with an arch around the large tourist masses of the Damrak and the Kalverstraat along the most beautiful spots of the city. We take a look at the former Marineterrein, stop at the most charming square in the city, cycle under the Rijksmuseum and eat a snack in De Hallen in Amsterdam-West. Via the Jordaan we go to North, where it happens nowadays, to conclude with a movie or a drink with a beautiful view over the IJ.

'De Dam'

The most visited square in Amsterdam is probably Dam Square. This is mainly due to the central location in the center of Amsterdam. At the Dam there are a number of special buildings such as the Royal Palace and the New Church. Also here is the National Monument.

Every year the National Commemoration of the Dead is held here on 4 May. The famous NH Krasnapolsky hotel, the Bijenkorf department store and the Madame Tussauds wax museum are located along the Dam. At De Dam you will regularly find street artists. The quality of this varies greatly.
